What is Money Muling?

Money muling is a technique used by criminals to launder money through third‑party accounts. A “money mule” receives funds from a scam victim or other source and then forwards them, often across several accounts, to hide the trail.

  • Money mules may be knowingly or unknowingly involved.
  • Typical red flags: rapid small transfers, many accounts, short‑lived activity.
  • Detection often uses transaction graphs, timing features, and behavioral patterns.
